Velika

Velika is located in Southern Arun, Velika Outskirts and is the capital city of the humans and serves as the base of operations for the Valkyon Federation. Its nickname is the "City of Wheels." Its patron goddess is Velik.



When players come to Velika through the Pegasus, they'll arrive at the Pegasus Platform. When they teleport to Velika, they will arrive at the Ring Plaza teleportal.
