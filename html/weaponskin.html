WEAPON SKIN

Weapon skins can be applied to weapons used by their respective operators. They can be purchased with Renown R6S-renown-icon and/or Credits R6S-credits-icon and can also be awarded through special events or the completion of certain actions. Skins are only available for the weapon they were purchased for unless they are a Special Tier Skin which are available for all weapons.
